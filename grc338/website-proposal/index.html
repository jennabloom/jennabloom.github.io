Introduction

Jenna’s Blooms

Jenna's Blooms is a small, locally owned flower shop with one location in downtown SLO. The company uses the physical store and a website, which customers can also order flowers off of.

Target audience

The target audience is not limited to one generation or age group, as flowers are for everyone. However, our company has a trendy, fresh vibe so will be aiming mainly for millennials - particularly women. They have a well-paying salary and aren’t afraid to splurge, as our products are high end.

Our target audience’s primary goals are either buying flowers on a special occasion for significant others, friends and family members, or getting flowers for an event. A large portion of our clients are people getting married who seek flowers for a wedding.
